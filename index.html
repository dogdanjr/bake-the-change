Why eight workstreams, not one list

Each workstream can move on its own — sponsorship outreach doesn't wait on volunteer recruitment, and social content doesn't wait on logistics. They only need to converge twice: Workstream 1's sponsor logos feed Workstream 5's signage, and Workstream 6's mission materials feed both Workstream 4's content and Workstream 5's table signage. Everything else runs in parallel.

The name itself is still open — Bake the Change is the working title, one of ten options Constance will choose from.
